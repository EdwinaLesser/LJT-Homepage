This work presents C-Eval, a large-scale evaluation suite designed to assess the capabilities of foundation models in Chinese. C-Eval includes a diverse set of tasks spanning multiple difficulty levels and disciplines, providing a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating model performance on Chinese language understanding, reasoning, and knowledge.
