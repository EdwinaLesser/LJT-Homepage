This work identifies and addresses the perception bottleneck in vision-language models (VLMs) for chart understanding. We conduct a comprehensive analysis of how VLMs process visual chart data and propose a novel framework to enhance their ability to extract meaningful information from charts, leading to significant improvements in chart understanding tasks.
