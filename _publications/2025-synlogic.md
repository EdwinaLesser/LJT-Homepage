This paper introduces SynLogic, a novel approach to synthesize large-scale verifiable reasoning data for training large language models (LLMs) to improve their logical reasoning capabilities. We demonstrate that our synthetic data can significantly enhance LLM performance on various logical reasoning benchmarks, outperforming existing methods that rely on human-annotated data.
