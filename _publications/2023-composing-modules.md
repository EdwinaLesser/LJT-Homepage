This work introduces a novel approach to compose parameter-efficient modules (PEMs) using simple arithmetic operations. We demonstrate that this method allows for the efficient combination of pre-trained PEMs to adapt large language models (LLMs) to new tasks, reducing the need for extensive re-training and improving the flexibility of model adaptation.
